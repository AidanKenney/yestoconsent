Who We Are

We knew sexuality education was lacking. In 2015, we decided to do something about it.

At YES! we are committed to providing high-quality, sex-positive, consent-based education to youth about every aspect of sexuality. Through our workshops, we expand participants' understanding of sexuality and provide them with tools to be more communicative, respectful, and safe.

We teach our workshops in a variety of settings, including public school, private schools, and churches with children and adults of all ages. In addition to teaching, we promote sex positive education through our blog, social media, and YouTube channel.

Our Team

Isy Abraham Raveson

Co-Founder

Isy graduated from Williams College with a degree in Women's, Gender, and Sexuality Studies. After college, she was trained as a sexuality educator by the Masakhane Center in Newark, NJ where she taught sex-positive sex ed to middle and high school students. Last year, she presented her original research on the intersections of sexuality education, shame, and rape culture at the Annual Conference of the American Association of Sexuality Educators, Counselors, and Therapists. She also has experience educating adults through her series of workshops about How to Talk to Kids about Sex. She currently works as a preschool teacher and a sexuality educator in Philadelphia.
