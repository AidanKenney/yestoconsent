Resources

If you are a survivor of sexual violence, you deserve support and access to groups that might help. We also have resources that provide information and support for those of us who identify as queer and trans, as well as resources for general sex positive sexuality information. If you need help and don't find anything that caters to your needs on this page, please contact us! our email is contact{AT}yestoconsent{DOT}org, and you will get a response directly from Rebecca, Rachel, Isy, or Natalie within 24 hours.
